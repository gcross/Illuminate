 NEAR_ABS [≈] (absolutely approximately equal)
 

These macros check that two expressions are equal in value within an absolute tolerance.

Specifically, x_NEAR_ABS_y(a,b,c) checks that \(|a-b| \le c\).


 NEAR_REL [≈] (relatively approximately equal)
 

These macros check that two expressions are equal in value within a relative tolerance.

Specifically, x_NEAR_REL_y(a,b,c) checks that either \(|a|+|b| \le c\) (that is, both a and b are approximately 0) or that \(\frac{|a-b|}{(|a|+|b|)/2} \le c\).
